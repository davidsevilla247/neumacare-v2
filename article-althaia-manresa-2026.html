NeumaCare's First Real-World Usability Test — At Hospital Althaia In Manresa

This morning, we traded our offices for the wards of Hospital Althaia in Manresa. And we couldn't be happier.

Our First Usability Test In A Real Hospital Environment

We carried out our first usability test in a real hospital environment. At NeumaCare, we believe technology only makes sense if it's genuinely useful for people — so being there and hearing direct feedback from healthcare staff was an incredible milestone for us.

Grateful To Althaia For Making This Possible

Thank you so much to Althaia, Xarxa Assistencial Universitària de Manresa, for welcoming us, for trusting entrepreneurial talent, and for helping us shape the future of hospital care. It's a true privilege to work with institutions this committed.

"It's a true privilege to work with institutions this committed."

— NeumaCare Team
