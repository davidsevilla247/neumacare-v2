NeumaCare at the Reus Start-Ups Summit — Closing REDESSA's Incubation Program

Today, we had the opportunity to present NeumaCare at the Reus Start-Ups Summit.

Selected Among 9 Finalists

NeumaCare was selected among the 9 finalist startups of the 5th edition of REDESSA's incubation program, and it was a privilege to be part of a day filled with so much entrepreneurial talent.

Gratitude To The Investor Ecosystem

Thank you to Joan Cavallé, Merce Tell, Roger Almuni and Marta Alsinella for their valuable insights into the investor ecosystem, and to Enric Ginovart Monclús for sharing his experience with Dormideo.

Thank You, REDESSA

A special thank you to Daniel Duran for believing in us from the very beginning and supporting us throughout this journey. Thanks also to the entire REDESSA team, and especially to Carles Prats, Josep Baiges and Eduard Vicente, for making us feel at home.

It was also a pleasure to share the stage with great entrepreneurs and inspiring projects such as SAENSA Innovation, AlgaNova Biotech, ¿Qué Plan?, IDZ – IngreDiZymes, Negociator.ai, New Art Foundation, Drop It, and VillageHub.

"With this day, we successfully close our time in REDESSA's incubation program — an intense, enriching experience full of learnings."

— NeumaCare Team
