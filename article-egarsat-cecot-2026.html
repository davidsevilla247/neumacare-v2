NeumaCare Meets Hospital Egarsat And Presents To Its Leadership At Cecot

Last week, our CMO Joan Jarque visited Hospital Egarsat as part of Cecot's health acceleration program for healthtech startups. A few days later, we presented the NeumaCare proposal to their leadership team at Cecot's headquarters, led by our CEO David Sevilla.

A Rewarding Visit To Hospital Egarsat

Our CMO, Joan Jarque, visited Hospital Egarsat as part of Cecot's health acceleration program for healthtech startups. We left with a lot of energy — useful conversations and truly enriching debates, including with Alberto Sancho, of the kind that remind you why you're building what you're building.

Thank you to the medical team who showed us the facilities and patiently answered every question that came up, and to Judit Pañella for accompanying us throughout the visit, and to Cecot for making it possible.

A few days later, it was time to present — and we couldn't have been more excited.

Presenting To Egarsat's Leadership At Cecot

Today, we presented NeumaCare's proposal to Hospital Egarsat's leadership team at the headquarters of Cecot, led by our CEO David Sevilla.

It was a highly rewarding experience, warmly received by the Egarsat team.

Learning From Those Who Know Hospitals Best

We want to especially thank Gemma Andreu, Alberto Sancho and Angeles Tuells for their participation, the interest they showed, and the positive feedback they shared with us.

For us, it is truly exciting to be able to put NeumaCare in front of professionals who know hospital day-to-day work up close, and who help us keep developing our technology.

"We keep moving forward."

— NeumaCare Team
