Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how NEUMACARE SYSTEMS, SOCIEDAD LIMITADA processes the personal data of users of this website, in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 on Data Protection and Digital Rights Guarantee (LOPDGDD).

1. Data Controller

Legal name: NEUMACARE SYSTEMS, SOCIEDAD LIMITADA

Trade name: NeumaCare

NIF: B26636514

Registered address: Carrer Rivera Sans, s/n, 43890 L'Hospitalet de l'Infant, Tarragona, Spain

Contact: davidsevilla@neumacare.cat (David Sevilla, Director / legal representative)

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect the personal data that users voluntarily provide through this website:

  • Contact form: full name, email address, organization or facility (optional), and the content of your message.
  • Newsletter subscription: email address.
  • Technical navigation data collected automatically by the hosting service (e.g. browser type, approximate location), used solely for the security and technical operation of the website.

3. Purpose of Processing

We use this data to respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form, to send newsletter updates to those who subscribe, and to maintain the security and proper functioning of the website. We do not use this data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Legal Basis

The legal basis for processing is the consent given by the user when submitting the contact form or subscribing to the newsletter (Article 6.1.a GDPR).

5. Data Retention

Data submitted through the contact form is retained only for as long as necessary to handle the inquiry and, where applicable, to maintain the resulting business relationship. Newsletter email addresses are retained until the user unsubscribes or requests deletion.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure of your data.
  • Object to or request restriction of processing.
  • Portability of your data, and to withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent prior to its withdrawal.

7. How to Exercise Your Rights

You may exercise these rights, or lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (Agencia Española de Protección de Datos, www.aepd.es), by contacting us at davidsevilla@neumacare.cat.

8. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except where required by law.

9. Cookies

This website uses only technical cookies necessary for its basic operation. It does not use analytics or advertising cookies that require prior consent.
